About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in high-consequence sectors.

We bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industry challenges through collaboration with universities and hands-on industry projects, ensuring professionals are ready for the complex risks of tomorrow.
